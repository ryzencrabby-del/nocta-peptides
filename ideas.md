# Nocta Peptides — Design Ideas

## Chosen Approach: Clinical Precision

**Design Movement:** Pharmaceutical / Biotech Premium (Amino Club reference)

**Core Principles:**
1. White-dominant, clinical, premium — no dark backgrounds anywhere
2. Typography-led hierarchy with Inter for body, tight tracking for headings
3. Dark navy (#1A3A4A) as the sole accent color for all interactive elements
4. Generous whitespace with subtle gray section breaks (#F7F7F7)

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: #FFFFFF pure white
- Section alternates: #F7F7F7 light gray
- Text primary: #0A0A0A near-black
- Text secondary: #6B7280 charcoal gray
- Accent/CTA: #1A3A4A dark navy
- Stars: #F5A623 gold
- Warning banner: #FFFBEB light yellow

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Sticky top nav with logo left, links center, cart right
- Asymmetric hero: text left, vial image right
- 4-column product grid on desktop
- Full-width trust/stat bars

**Signature Elements:**
1. Research warning banner on every page (yellow strip)
2. COA cards with purity percentage prominently displayed
3. Dosage selector pill buttons (navy when selected)

**Typography System:**
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal text-gray-600
- Labels: uppercase tracking-widest text-xs

**Animation:**
- Hover: subtle card lift (translateY -2px, shadow increase)
- Modal: fade in + scale up
- Cart drawer: slide from right
- Dosage selection: smooth color transition
